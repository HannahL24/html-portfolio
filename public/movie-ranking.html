Hannah's Favourite Movies

My top favourite movies of all time!

This is one of my favourite movies, with fantastic music and the plot of an emotional rollercoaster!

Toy Story

Taking my right back to my childhood, my favourite movie growing up was Toy Story. I love them all!

Back to the Future

Not only do I know most of it word for word, but Marty McFly inspired the name of my favourite band.

Harry Potter

It's too hard for me to choose a favourite - between The Chamber of Secrets, The Order of the Phoenix or The Goblet of Fire.
